Can you explain the ABCDE criteria for melanoma screening?

smart_toy

The ABCDE criteria is a recognized medical framework for early detection:

  • A (Asymmetry): One half doesn't match the other.
  • B (Border): Irregular, scalloped, or poorly defined edges.
  • C (Color): Variations of color (brown, black, tan, red, blue).
  • D (Diameter): Usually larger than 6mm (pencil eraser).
  • E (Evolving): Changing in size, shape, or color over time.
